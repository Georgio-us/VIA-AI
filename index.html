Project Overview

The Challenge

Our client, a premium real estate agency specializing in luxury properties, was struggling with an outdated website that couldn't showcase their high-end portfolio effectively. They needed a modern platform that would attract international buyers and streamline their property management process.

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive property management platform featuring advanced search capabilities, virtual tour integration, and a fully integrated CRM system. The solution was designed to handle multiple languages and currencies for their international clientele.
